# Meeting Request Template

## Structure
```
Subject: Meeting Request: [Topic/Project Name]

Dear [Recipient Name],

I hope this email finds you well. I would like to schedule a meeting to discuss [topic].

The purpose of our meeting would be to:
• [Key objective 1]
• [Key objective 2]
• [Key objective 3]

Would you be available for [duration] on [date options]? Please let me know what time works best for you.

Alternative times:
• [Date/Time 1]
• [Date/Time 2]

Thank you for your time and consideration. I look forward to connecting.

Best regards,
[Your Name]
[Your Title]
[Company/Department]
```

## When to Use
- When requesting a new meeting
- When proposing a discussion about specific topic
- When coordinating with team members
- When discussing project details

## Tips
- Be specific about meeting purpose
- Suggest multiple time options
- Keep initial request concise
- Include estimated duration
