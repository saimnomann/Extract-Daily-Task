# Follow-up Email Template

## Structure
```
Subject: Following Up: [Clear, specific subject]

Dear [Recipient Name],

Following up on my previous email regarding [topic/context].

I wanted to check if you've had a chance to [specific action]. If you need any additional information, please let me know.

The key points to consider are:
• [Point 1]
• [Point 2]
• [Point 3]

Could you please provide an update by [date]?

Thank you for your assistance.

Sincerely,
[Your Name]
[Your Title]
[Contact Information]
```

## When to Use
- When following up on a previous email
- When checking on status of a request
- When ensuring recipient has received important information
- When gentle reminder is needed

## Tips
- Wait 3-5 business days before following up
- Reference previous email specifically
- Keep tone polite and professional
- Provide clear deadline if appropriate
