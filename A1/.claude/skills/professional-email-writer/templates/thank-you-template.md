# Thank You Email Template

## Structure
```
Subject: Thank You for [Specific Action/Contribution]

Dear [Recipient Name],

Thank you for [specific action/reason/contribution].

I particularly appreciated [specific detail/example]. This was valuable because [explain impact/benefit].

Key takeaways from your [action/contribution]:
• [Insight 1]
• [Insight 2]
• [Insight 3]

I look forward to future opportunities to collaborate. Please don't hesitate to reach out if there's anything I can assist with.

Best regards,
[Your Name]
[Your Title]
[Company/Department]
```

## When to Use
- After receiving help or assistance
- When someone provides valuable feedback
- When acknowledging a contribution
- After a successful meeting or presentation

## Tips
- Be specific about what you're thanking them for
- Mention the impact of their contribution
- Keep it genuine and heartfelt
- Include specific details when possible
